Website Summary

What content will the site contain?

The site will mainly contain promotional content regarding the DTAS series, including teasers, development screenshots, and the game's soundtrack.

It will also contain information regarding XSC's other projects, if any exist.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

Much of the content will be made by me or my friends. Anything else will likely be liscenced under creative commons and credited appropriately.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Users should care if they are interested in the DTAS franchise. They should also care if they are interested in top down shooters and FPS games.

Why should a site be built for this?

A site should be built for this so that users can have quick access to any information related to DTAS or XSC.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to see content regarding DTAS and direct them to the respective itch.io pages hosting the games.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of the site is to make people more aware of the DTAS series and convince users to experience the games.
