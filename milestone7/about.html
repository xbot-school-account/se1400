What is XSC?

XSCorporation (XSC) is a game studio founded by non-Euclidean cryptids from a dimension beyond human comprehension, doing their best to appear convincingly human. We create games that blend emotional storytelling with reality-warping mechanics and visuals that occasionally violate the laws of physics—purely by design, of course. Our titles explore the thresholds of perception, memory, and existential elasticity, offering players experiences that linger far beyond the end credits. Enjoy our catalog across platforms such as Xbox, PlayStation, PC, Omnibox 0, and other devices capable of sustaining dimensional stability.

While our origins lie beyond space, sanity, and traditional market segmentation, we remain committed to delivering innovative, soul-safe interactive experiences for life-forms restricted to the third dimension. Every project is crafted with care, calibrated for human neurological structures, and—more often than not—designed to be non-hazardous to standard cognitive frameworks. We welcome you to step into our worlds, even if you aren't equipped to see all of them at once. XSCorporation: We almost pass for human.

Our Mission Statement

At XSCorporation (XSC), our mission is to deliver truly immersive experiences that defy the boundaries of human understanding. We craft worlds that not only entertain, but subtly enlighten the mind, expanding perceptions beyond conventional limitations. Each of our projects is designed to blur the line between game and revelation, pulling players into intricate realities where the unexpected is not an obstacle, but a feature. With titles like DTAS, Cooperative Donjon 2, and Quarter Life, we have pioneered innovative, mind-bending gameplay that reflects our deep understanding of human desires, fears, and aspirations—knowledge gleaned through observation, interpretation, and a few methods best left undisclosed.

At the core of XSC lies a relentless drive to evolve, disrupt, and connect with players in ways that are... well, most would call "human." We tirelessly refine our craft, ensuring each experience resonates not just on the surface, but deep within the cognitive frameworks that define reality. Our vision isn’t just to entertain; it's to usher humanity toward a broader, stranger horizon—one carefully calibrated to feel thrillingly inevitable. Welcome to the future—our future.

A Tragedy in Flex and Fray: A Sonnet on Grid and Man

O Grid, thou lattice vile of cruel design,
Thy syntax speaks in tongues the gods forswore.
We draw thy lines, thy bounds, thy columned spine—
Yet chaos walks where order stood before.

"Repeat!" we cry, "Auto-fit, obey!"
But lo, our children squeeze into the void,
While spacing mocks us, drifting far away—
The right edge yawns, alignment is destroyed.

But fools are we, who seek in sheets of style
A scaffold for the cosmos we pretend.
What mad ambition builds this code-fraught pile?
What mortal thinks the DOM shall ever bend?

So curse not Grid alone—take up thy blame,
For Man and Markup both are much the same.
