What is XSC?

XSCorporation (XSC) is a game studio founded by non-Euclidean cryptids from a dimension beyond human comprehension, doing their best to appear convincingly human. We create games that blend emotional storytelling with reality-warping mechanics and visuals that occasionally violate the laws of physics—purely by design, of course. Enjoy our games on platforms like XBox, Playstation, PC, Omnibox 0, and more. While our origins lie beyond space and sanity, we’re committed to delivering innovative, soul-safe interactive experiences for life-forms restricted to the third dimension. XSCorporation: We almost pass for human.

Our Mission Statement

At XSCorporation (XSC), our mission is to deliver truly immersive experiences that defy the boundaries of human understanding. We craft worlds that not only entertain, but subtly enlighten the mind, expanding perceptions beyond conventional limitations. With titles like DTAS, Cooperative Donjon 2, and Quarter Life, we have pioneered innovative, mind-bending gameplay that reflects our deep understanding of human desires, fears, and aspirations. At the core of XSC is a relentless drive to evolve, disrupt, and connect with our players in ways that are... well, most people would call “human.” Welcome to the future—our future.

A Tragedy in Flex and Fray: A Sonnet on Grid and Man

O Grid, thou lattice vile of cruel design,
Thy syntax speaks in tongues the gods forswore.
We draw thy lines, thy bounds, thy columned spine—
Yet chaos walks where order stood before.

"Repeat!" we cry, "Auto-fit, obey!"
But lo, our children squeeze into the void,
While spacing mocks us, drifting far away—
The right edge yawns, alignment is destroyed.

But fools are we, who seek in sheets of style
A scaffold for the cosmos we pretend.
What mad ambition builds this code-fraught pile?
What mortal thinks the DOM shall ever bend?

So curse not Grid alone—take up thy blame,
For Man and Markup both are much the same.
